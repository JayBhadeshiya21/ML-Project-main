Clinical Inputs Used for Prediction

The model evaluates objective measurements, examination values, and lifestyle indicators.

Age

Captured in days and converted for model usage.

Height

Patient height in centimeters.

Weight

Patient weight in kilograms.

Gender

Encoded sex-based category.

Systolic Pressure

Arterial pressure during contraction (ap_hi).

Diastolic Pressure

Arterial pressure between beats (ap_lo).

Cholesterol

1 normal, 2 above normal, 3 high.

Glucose

1 normal, 2 above normal, 3 high.

Smoking

Binary smoking indicator.

Alcohol

Binary alcohol-use indicator.

Activity

Binary physical activity indicator.
